I immigrated to the US at the age of four and grew up with two completely different cultures. Those separate cultures became invaluable to my development, having multiple experiences to tap into allowed me to transfer ideas, skills, and find creative solutions. I believe in bridging cultures and using diversity and technology to solve our most difficult challenges.

Myself in three words: curiosity-driven development. I've always enjoyed learning about how things operate, how it's made, and what's happening behind the scenes. I'm very much driven by a need to understand moving parts. This has led me to study film/digital art in college, become certified in networking technologies, and now finally beginning a career as a web developer. Learning to code is exposing me to even more learning opportunities, exciting new technologies, and inspiring projects. Time to get dangerous!
